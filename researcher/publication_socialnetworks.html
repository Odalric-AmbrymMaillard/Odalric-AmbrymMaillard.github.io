🚀 Research Throwback! 🚀

I’m pleased to highlight a key publication from 2011: “Kullback-Leibler Upper Confidence Bounds for Optimal Sequential Allocation” by Olivier Cappé, Aurélien Garivier, Odalric-Ambrym Maillard, Rémi Munos, and Gilles Stoltz, published in the Annals of Statistics, 2013.

🌟 This paper was pivotal in analyzing the kl-UCB and empirical KL-UCB algorithms for optimal sequential allocation in stochastic multi-armed bandit models. Key insights include:

🔬 A generic index policy based on Kullback-Leibler divergence.
🧠 Unified finite-time analysis of the regret of these algorithms.
🔄 Applicability to both one-parameter exponential families and bounded distributions.

Our work has opened the road to modern analysis targeting exact instance-dependent asymptotic optimality.
