Publications

In this annoted publication list, I clarify my role as a contributor using simple font conventions and encourage everyone to use them in their publication list. Indeed it is virtually impossible to understand the contributing role to an article from the mere position in the author list alone (seriously, who imagined that?): the rules for ordering the authors vary from place to place, communities, your role as a supervisor or not, plus various ad-hoc rules. In many cases, the alphabetical order is considered, mixed together with academic positions and seniority, which gives little clue on the actual contributing role of an author.
